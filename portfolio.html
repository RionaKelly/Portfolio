Portfolio

Here are the past projects that I have worked on either in college, events, or in my own time.
I'll be updating this whenever I have a new projects that I want to show off so that you can all ready more about it and hopefully try it out!

Games

My Desktop Pet

This is a simple virtual pet that will live on your desktop and you can raise and take care of while using your computer. I am currently working on this full time at it is my project for this college semester. While it is still a work in progress you can download beta versions here and to try it out!

An Phiast in: Fishy Business

This is my team's Game Sting submisson for the 2026 Dingle Animation Festival! A short comedic detective game where An Phiast must try to solve the mystery of who stole 'The Perfect Fish and Chips'. I worked on design with my team, dialogue, menus, and modelling an area.

Starburst

My friend and I's entry to the 2025 Games Fleadh, Starburst, is a fast paced 2D Shooter where you grab asteroids flying past you you and throw them towards planets to destroy them and harvest their cores. We were awarded Best Game using Libraries and Best Game Mechanics at the event.

Unmasked

A short narrative game that I made with my team for the 2026 Global Game Jam about helping village people who have been cursed by a witch to wear masks that represent their fears and insecurities. Play as a wizard therapist and explore the psyches of the townspeople, and break the curse.

Gold Divers

A board game that I made with my team for the 2025 Global Game Jam with my college. You must explore the depths by placing random tiles to try and find treasure to bring back up to the surface.

Paint Party

Paint Party is a multiplayer party game using SFML I made in college with a group, where the players must cover as much area as they can in paint to win. I coded all of the gameplay while my teammates made the assets and menus.
